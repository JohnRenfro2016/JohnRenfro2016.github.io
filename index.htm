Transcribing

One great way to study a foreign language is to do this. For Google Chrome browser, dictation.io allows you to get real time subtitles for many languages. Just turn your microphone volume up all the way. Go to a foreign streaming television show and then press "Start". Install the Google Translate extension on Chrome. Highlight the subtitles and you will get it translated into English.

Memorizing Kanji

  • Use 10ten Japanese Reader browser extension to read kanji on your desktop or laptop computer.
  • Group kanji by the same sound using WaniKani.
  • Group them by similar elements.
  • Practice writing only same sound Kanji until you can do it from memory using Android Akebi.
