Japanese Language Study
The only difficult parts of pronouncing Japanese are the long syllables. If there is no 'U' at the end of a syllable, pronounce it like you would a command in English. Wait!, Stop!. Otherwise, pronounce the syllables in an even tone.
WaniKani is for looking up same sound Japanese characters.
Akebi for Android is for practicing writing those same sound Japanese characters from memory.
The Japanese to English Parser is my application for studying Japanese grammar.
