Russian Language Study
I just don't see the point continuing the Russian to English parser. It's probably the easiest language for English speakers to learn. Learn to read Cyrillic. Go to YouTube and learn what the prepositions and suffixes mean. Many words are shared from the original Greek. You can guess many of them. TransOver for Firefox has really amazing contextual machine translation. Highlight the prepositional phrases. Google Translate is the other important part of learning Russian. Type simple English sentences and listen to and repeat the Russian translations.
