Grammar Terms

Suffixes have multiple meanings. Try to match the words like this.
nomi-verb-accu-dati
If there is a passive verb, match accu-verb-nomi-dati
prep-prep-prep-prep
plur-plur-plur-plur

(accu)=accusative case,the direct object
(adje)=adjective
(anim)=animated living things
(dati)=dative case,the indirect object,to who or what
(geni)=genitive case, possessive, whose things, of what
(inan)=inanimated non-living things
(plur)=plural
(nomi)=nominative case,the subject,as for who or what
(sing)=singular
